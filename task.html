AI Tools Every Student Should Know

AI is transforming how students learn, write, and stay productive. Here are three powerful tools that every student should explore.

  • ChatGPT – for study help, coding, summaries, and explanations
  • Grammarly – improve your writing, grammar, and tone
  • Notion AI – for organizing notes and boosting productivity
  • QuillBot – Paraphrasing and grammar improvement for writing tasks.
  • Perplexity AI – AI-powered research with citations.
  • Tome – Create professional presentations instantly with AI.
  • Otter.ai – Record and transcribe lectures and group study sessions.
  • Canva Magic Write – Generate creative content while designing.
